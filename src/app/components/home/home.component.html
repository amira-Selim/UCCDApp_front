About Us

At the University Center for Career Development (UCCD), we strive to empower students and graduates to build a successful career path by equipping them with the skills required in the labor market and providing an effective link between them and top companies.

Services for Students

Resume Review

We help you write and develop your resume professionally to highlight your skills.

Mock Interviews

Practical sessions to prepare you to pass personal interviews with confidence.

Training Workshops

Specialized courses in soft and technical skills required in the labor market.

Career Guidance

We provide summer internships and job opportunities through our partners.

Services for Employers

Job Posting

An integrated platform to post jobs and internships, reaching thousands of students.

Talent Discovery

Access a massive database of outstanding graduates to select the best fit.

Application Management

An easy dashboard to review resumes and communicate with applicants efficiently.
